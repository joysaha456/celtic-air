SHIPPING & DELIVERY

How can I see what I have purchased in the past? We’ve made checking on your previous orders easy. Simply log in to your account to view your previous purchases.

I live outside the UK can I still order from the site? We Deliver to the US, UK, Europe, China, Asia, The Middle East, Africa, Australia, New Zealand and other destinations. For more detailed information on destinations we deliver to and delivery costs please visit our delivery & handling page. Please note that delivery charges may vary depending on where your parcel is travelling to, we cannot deliver to Russian PO boxes. We may need to calculate your shipping at a special rate after you submit and order. Special rates may increase or lower your shipping cost. If a special shipping rate needs to be applied to your parcel, no order will be processed until you have agreed to the special order rate.

How much is the delivery charge?

Delivery charge varies depending on the value and destination of your parcel. Please see here.

Is VAT included?

We include VAT on all items on our website however this may removed at the Order Summary page depending on your delivery country. Your taxes will be shown on the Order Summary page during checkout. When you complete your order, the final tax charged will be present on your order confirmation.

How long will it take for my order to arrive?

How long it will take for your order to arrive depends on the destination it has to travel to. Please see here.

Can I specify a delivery time?

Unfortunately, we cannot specify an exact time, however within the UK our items will usually be delivered between 8am and 6pm.

PRIVACY & SECURITY

How secure are my details?

Your privacy is of the utmost importance to us. Please be assured that we never pass your details on to a third party. We use industry standard encryption on our website where all communication between you and our site is secure. We take every step possible to keep your financial information private and safe. If you have further questions about the security of your details, please do not hesitate to contact one of our Customer Care advisors.

RETURNS & REPLACEMENTS

Can I cancel or amend my order?

Changed your mind? Not a problem, but please contact us as soon as you realise that you need to change the details of your order. Please note that if your order has already been processed, it may be too late to cancel or change the delivery. If this is the case please be rest assured that we will be happy for you to return it once it has been received.

ORDERING

How do I track my order?

Each time you order through our website, you will receive two emails from us:

  • An order acknowledgement email should arrive in your inbox immediately letting you know your order was received.
  • A dispatch confirmation email, including tracking details will be sent to alert you when your order leaves our warehouse. This email will let you know that your order is on its way to you. The excellent news is if you are registered with us is you can track the status of your order simply by logging in to your account. If you have any queries about the location of your parcel please contact one our Customer Service Advisors who will be happy to help.
